## Design Context

### Users

MythosMUD players interact in long-form, text-forward sessions where rapid command input, readable state, and
continuous situational awareness are essential. They need to parse room state, combat feedback, chat, and system
events quickly while staying emotionally engaged in a cosmic-horror world.

### Brand Personality

Scholarly occult, foreboding, and immersive. The interface should evoke unsettling atmosphere and ritual seriousness
without becoming chaotic or hard to read. Emotional target is immersion-first atmospheric horror.

### Aesthetic Direction

Dark terminal-first visual language with restrained neon accents, high-contrast typography, and layered panel depth.
Motion should feel uncanny and atmospheric, not playful. Visual references should favor signal-rich command consoles
and eldritch ambience; avoid glossy consumer-app aesthetics or bright, cheerful palettes.

### Design Principles

- Atmosphere before ornament: every visual decision should deepen tension and mythos immersion.
- Legibility under pressure: critical game state must remain instantly scannable during intense moments.
- Ritualized interaction: commands, feedback, and transitions should feel deliberate and arcane.
- Cohesive darkness: maintain consistent dark surfaces, restrained accent use, and low visual noise.
- Accessible dread: preserve WCAG-conscious contrast and focus affordances while sustaining horror tone.
